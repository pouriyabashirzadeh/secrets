## Overview

This comprehensive guide provides actionable strategies, tools, and templates for generating B2B leads and building meaningful connections in the Iranian market.

## Part 1: Lead Generation Strategy

### Target Customer Profile (ICP - Ideal Customer Profile)

**Primary Target:**
- **Company Size**: 50-500 employees
- **Industries**: Corporate offices, private schools, clinics, tech companies
- **Location**: Tehran, Isfahan, Shiraz, Mashhad
- **Decision Makers**: HR Managers, Office Managers, Procurement Officers, CEOs (small companies)
- **Budget**: 50,000,000 - 300,000,000 IRR annually for uniforms
- **Pain Points**: Quality issues, unreliable suppliers, poor service

**Secondary Target:**
- **Company Size**: 20-50 employees (easier to close, smaller orders)
- **Company Size**: 500+ employees (longer sales cycle, larger contracts)

## Part 3: Lead Management System

### CRM Setup (Simple Excel Version)

**Lead Tracking Spreadsheet:**

| Lead ID | Date Added | Company | Contact Name | Title | Phone | Email | Source | Status | Next Action | Notes |
|---------|------------|---------|--------------|-------|-------|-------|--------|--------|-------------|-------|
| L001 | 2025-11-11 | ABC Corp | John Smith | HR Mgr | 09123456789 | john@abc.com | LinkedIn | Qualified | Send proposal | Needs 50 uniforms |

**Status Categories:**
- **New**: Just added to system
- **Contacted**: Initial outreach made
- **Qualified**: Confirmed need and budget
- **Proposal Sent**: Waiting for decision
- **Negotiation**: Discussing terms
- **Closed-Won**: Customer!
- **Closed-Lost**: Didn't convert
- **Nurture**: Not ready now, follow up later

### Lead Scoring System

**Score leads 1-10 based on:**
- **Company Size** (1-3 points): Larger = higher score
- **Decision-Maker Access** (1-2 points): Direct access = 2
- **Timeline** (1-3 points): Immediate need = 3, 6+ months = 1
- **Budget** (1-2 points): Budget confirmed = 2

**Priority:**
- 8-10 points: High priority (contact within 24 hours)
- 5-7 points: Medium priority (contact within 3 days)
- 1-4 points: Low priority (nurture campaign)

### Follow-Up Schedule

**New Lead:**
- Day 0: Initial contact
- Day 1: Follow-up if no response
- Day 3: Second follow-up
- Day 7: Final follow-up
- Day 30: Move to nurture campaign

**Qualified Lead:**
- Day 0: Send proposal
- Day 3: Follow-up call
- Day 7: Check-in email
- Day 14: Final push
- Day 30: Move to nurture or closed-lost

**Nurture Campaign:**
- Monthly: Valuable content email
- Quarterly: Check-in call
- Special: Event invitations, promotions

## Conclusion

Lead generation is a numbers game combined with relationship building. The key is:

1. **Consistency**: Daily outreach activities
2. **Persistence**: Multiple touchpoints per lead
3. **Value**: Always provide value, not just sales pitches
4. **Follow-up**: Most sales happen after 5-7 touchpoints
5. **Tracking**: Measure everything and optimize

Start with the channels that feel most comfortable, then expand. Focus on quality conversations, not just quantity of leads.

**Remember**: Every "no" gets you closer to a "yes". Keep going!
