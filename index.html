Crisis Preparedness: Comprehensive protocols and communication templates to handle 6 common crisis scenarios and protect your reputation.

6 Crisis Scenarios Covered

1. Quality Issues

Response: Acknowledge within 24 hours, investigate, replace defective items, offer compensation, implement corrective measures

Severity Levels: Low (<10% affected), Medium (10-30%), High (>30% or safety concerns)

2. Delivery Delays

Response: Inform immediately when delay is known, explain honestly, provide new timeline, offer alternatives/compensation

Prevention: Build buffer time, communicate proactively, maintain backup suppliers

3. Customer Dissatisfaction

Response: Listen and empathize, understand issue, take ownership, propose solutions, follow through and exceed expectations

Key: Turn unhappy customer into loyal advocate through exceptional recovery

4. Negative Online Review

Response: Respond publicly within 24 hours, apologize, take offline, resolve privately, follow up publicly with resolution

Goal: Show others you care and take action

5. Supplier Failure

Response: Assess impact, contact backup suppliers, inform customers proactively, develop contingency plan

Prevention: Multiple suppliers, regular audits, buffer inventory

6. Financial/Payment Issues

Response: Review documentation, understand concern, provide clear breakdown, find mutually acceptable solution

Approach: Flexibility and understanding while protecting business interests

The 5 A's of Crisis Response

  1. Acknowledge - Recognize issue immediately
  2. Apologize - Sincere, specific, no excuses
  3. Assess - Understand full situation
  4. Act - Take immediate corrective action
  5. Assure - Explain prevention, rebuild trust

Communication Guidelines

Do's:

  • Respond quickly (within 24 hours)
  • Be honest and transparent
  • Take responsibility
  • Focus on solutions
  • Follow through on promises

Don'ts:

  • Ignore or delay response
  • Make excuses or blame others
  • Get defensive or argumentative
  • Make promises you can't keep
  • Discuss publicly (take offline)

Response Time Standards

  • Positive feedback: Thank within 24 hours
  • Questions: Respond within 4 hours (business hours)
  • Complaints: Acknowledge within 2 hours, resolve within 24 hours
  • Crisis: Immediate response (within 1 hour)

Crisis Prevention Checklist

  • Rigorous quality control processes
  • Clear, realistic timelines
  • Proactive communication
  • Backup suppliers and contingency plans
  • Regular customer check-ins
  • Documentation of all processes

Reputation Monitoring

  • Google Alerts for brand mentions
  • Social media monitoring
  • Customer feedback tracking
  • Weekly manual checks
  • Quarterly reputation audits
