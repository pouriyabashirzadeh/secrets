# Market Research: B2B Clothing Supply Industry in Iran

## Executive Summary

The Iranian B2B clothing and uniform market represents a significant opportunity with estimated market size of $2-3 billion USD annually. The market is characterized by strong demand from corporate, educational, healthcare, and government sectors, with increasing emphasis on quality, customization, and reliable supply chains.
